Light roasted coffees are roasted the least amount of time. As a result, light roasts will have more brightness, more complexity, and more fruit-forward, herbal flavors. Light roasts will have more caffeine than medium or dark roasts.

Medium roast coffee has a little thicker body than light roasts, and carry a much more balanced flavor with a medium amount of caffeine.

Dark roast coffee beans stay on the roasting machine for a longer time or at a higher temperature. This means the beans will have a more single-note flavor and less complexity.
